# BaristaCats ?☕ Remake

> Originally submitted as a Global Game Jam 2024 entry, this version brings in some features that were cut from the original to time constraints, sleep deprivation, and not enough caffeine available to the team.

## Welcome... no, no, no... brace yourself for 'BaristaCats'. 

I'm Mr. Milky, your... oh, the horror! I'm a cat, a simple, domesticated cat, thrust into the relentless grind of a barista's life. The espresso machine? It's a hissing serpent, ready to strike. The cups? They clatter and crash, echoing the shattering of my sanity. The orders? A relentless tsunami, each 'extra hot', 'no foam', 'double shot' a nail in my coffin.

The workload is a monstrous, insatiable beast, the customers an unending, merciless horde, the pressure a crushing, unbearable weight. I'm trapped in an apron, my skills a mockery, my fear a constant companion. The coffee pot, oh the coffee pot, its impending emptiness a metaphor for my soul. So, if you dare, join me in this jittery, heart-pounding, caffeine-fueled nightmare. It's... it's... oh whiskers, I can't do this anymore! I'm a cat, for crying out loud, not a barista! I need a nap, a ball of yarn, a simple life!

This... this is too much!

## Controls

- **Movement**: WASD/Arrow Keys
- **Interaction**: E
- **Meow**: Q

## Order Cheat Sheet:

- **Americatto** - *standard plain coffee* - grind some beans, brew the coffee in the coffee machine, and serve.
- **Catte** - *milky coffee* - grind some beans, brew the coffee, grab correct milk and froth it like a true barista, serve.

## Help

- **Grinder** - Interact with the grinder to grind the beans, keep em' grinding until done. Interact with the coffee machine to brew a nice cup of black coffee (don't question milky holding the grinder, he hasn't figured out yet that he can take just the tray).
- **Coffee Machine** - Interact with coffee beans to brew up to 3 cups on the go. Interact with milk in hand, to froth the milk and add it to a complete brew. Interact with nothing in your hand to collect a cup of black coffee.
- **Milk Fridge** - Interact with the milk fridge to grab the correct milk - make sure you grab the correct milk!
- **Bin** - Interact with the bin to get rid of any item currently in your hand.
- **Cash Register** - Interact to serve your perfectly brewed coffee.
